Nancy Yacovzada

Research Scientist · Machine Learning · Biomedical Analytics
nancy.yaco@gmail.com

I am a research scientist and machine learning expert working at the interface of data science, biology, and medicine. My work focuses on biomedical analytics, multi-omics biomarkers, digital health, and personalized medicine.

I completed my PhD at the Weizmann Institute of Science, where I combined machine learning with genetics, molecular data, and large-scale electronic health records to advance disease diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment response.

Early in my career I led software and data teams - building full-stack/server systems, NLP, and real-time optimization (RTB) for production - before shifting to biomedical ML, causal inference, and interdisciplinary research.
